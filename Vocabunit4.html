Unit 4 Vocab
Culture A group of belief systems and practices by people.
Folk Culture A culture with Homogenous population and are typically rural and is cohesive in cultural traits.
Popular Culture A culture with Heterogenous population and are typically Urban and experiences quickly changing traits.
Local Culture A group of people who see themselves as a collective group of people or a community. People who share experiences, traits and more.
Homogenous Same or alike.
Heterogenous Different.
Hierarchical Diffusion The spread of a trend or topic through something.
Cultural Hearth Place where culture originated (home.)
Assimilate When another culture interacts with another culture one culture will loose it’s trait. Close to adaptation.
Custom A routine or practice that someone does daily.
Cultural appropriation When other cultures adopt a culture in order to use their knowledge for their own benefit.
Taboo Something that is frowned upon.
Ethnic Neighborhood A neighborhood that can be near a large city or out in the middle of no where, mostly a local culture
Invasion and Succession When one social group moves to a different area.
Authenticity Something that is or has the quality of being original or unique.
Distance Decay The further away from the hearth the less interaction.
Time-space compression How quickly interlinked places diffuse, and how two places are linked through transportation.
Reterritorialization When people begin to produce an aspect of popular culture.
Cultural landscape The visual imprint of human activity on a landscape.
Place lessness When a place has lost it’s uniqueness of place in the cultural landscape.
